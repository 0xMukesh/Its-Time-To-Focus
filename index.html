It's Time to Focus

A online student friendly application made to help the students to stay productive and focused during this online study age

Birth of It's Time To Focus ðŸ”¥

It's Time To Focus was originally created by Kira, while he was bored sitting in room wondering how can he kill his time, surfing through his discord mentions
He saw that most of his discord friends where struggling to stay focused during this online study age, at this specific moment an idea struck into his mind of making a web application which helps the students
He opened Visual Studio Code and created a file named index.html and this was the birth of It's Time To Focus
"It's Time To Focus" originally started with a simple basic clock on the homepage and eventually new features were added such as Pomodoro clock, To-do list, Lofi Music player and many more feature will be coming... ðŸ”¥
